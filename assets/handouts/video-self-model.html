Video‑Based Self‑Model

What: Record the pupil doing the steps (or edit to success), then replay to build confidence and recall.

  1. Capture successful attempts (even partial) on video.
  2. Trim to 15–45 s; remove distractions.
  3. Watch → practise immediately → reinforce.
  4. Update weekly as skills improve.
