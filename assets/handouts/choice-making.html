Choice‑Making Interventions

What: Offer structured choices (order, tool, partner, place) to increase engagement and reduce avoidance.

  1. Decide 2 acceptable options for each step.
  2. Use clear visuals or words; honour the choice.
  3. Keep choices brief; avoid overwhelming menus.
  4. Fade frequency once engagement stabilises.
