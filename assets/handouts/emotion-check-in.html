Emotion Check‑In & Scales

What: A quick “how I feel” rating (colours or numbers) paired with a pre‑agreed regulation tool.

  1. Pick a simple scale (e.g., Green/Yellow/Red).
  2. Pre‑teach 1 tool per colour (e.g., headphones, walk, breathing).
  3. Check‑in at set times; log briefly.
  4. Review weekly; adjust tools.
