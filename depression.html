Depression / Low Mood

Small, repeatable steps help restart energy and rhythm. Combine light movement with breath pacing. Keep goals tiny and consistent.

Daily rhythm sketch

  • Morning: 3–5 minutes of Coherent 5‑5 while standing at a window.
  • Mid‑day: short outdoor walk; match steps to a 4‑4 or 5‑5 breath.
  • Evening: 4‑7‑8 for sleep onset (2–4 rounds, not too many).

When stuck

  • Start with one breath. Then choose the next step only.
  • Use “could I do 1 minute?” rather than “should I do 20?”

If there is risk of harm or thoughts of suicide, seek local urgent support immediately.
